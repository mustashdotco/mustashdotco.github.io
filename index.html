"Subscriptions in one place"

mustash.co is a hub to manage all your subscriptions

Now a days you can buy virtually everything with a subscription. In an effort to make life easier for consumers, many companies are offering subscription services for music, movies, car share, software, personal care, vitamins, groceries, clothing, and anything else you could imagine. Many startups are building business solely on this model.

 

Consumers now have more options and

trying to manage more subscriptions than ever.

 

But how often do we continue to pay for subscription we no longer use? How many times have we tried to cancel a subscription and cannot find how to do so? Or maybe our credit card recently expired and that is why we have not received our latest order.

 

This is why we created to Mustash, the only hub you need to manage all your subscriptions in one place. Mustash is a plug in for Mint.com that allow users to see all their subscriptions in one place. Users can see how many subscriptions they currently have, what cards are paying for the services, how long they have had the subscription, how long until the subscription expires, and how much is being spent on all subscriptions.

 

We are here to help you continue with the subscriptions you love and manage them in order to optimise their use.
